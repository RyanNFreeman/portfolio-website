About Ryan Freeman

An English major from Brigham Young University, I excel in delivering relevant content and polished style. My childhood was surrounded by siblings--seven, if fact. My father is a professor and my mother a storyteller with a masters degree in folklore. I learned from a young age that everyone loves a good story. Delivery, on the other hand, can be difficult. With my experience and expertise, I can help tell your company's story.
